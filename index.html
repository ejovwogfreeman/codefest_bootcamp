What is Codefest Summer Bootcamp?

Codefest Summer Bootcamp is an exciting 2-week STEM training and innovation program designed to empower kids and teens (ages 10–16) with real-world skills in coding, artificial intelligence, robotics, and creative problem-solving. This is the 7th edition. It's more than just learning — it's a journey into the future!

A fun, hands-on tech experience for kids & teens (ages 10–16) to learn coding, AI, robotics, and showcase their creativity at the Innovation Expo!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do kids need prior coding experience?

No — we begin with foundational concepts, making it easy for absolute beginners, while also including advanced topics and challenges tailored to intermediate and experienced participants to ensure everyone grows.

Is the program safe and supervised?

Yes — participants are guided by experienced instructors in a safe, supportive environment. Every session is closely monitored to ensure full supervision, comfort, and a productive learning experience for all.

Are there take-home projects?

Yes! Every participant will complete and take home at least one project they built from scratch, showcasing their new skills and creativity, and reinforcing what they learned during the bootcamp.

Can parents attend the Expo?

Absolutely! It's a family-friendly celebration where everyone—parents, kids, and guests—can enjoy a safe, welcoming environment filled with fun activities, learning opportunities, and engaging tech experiences together.
